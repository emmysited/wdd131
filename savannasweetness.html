Savanna Sweetness Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Savanna Sweetness

This name represents a brand dedicated to promoting high-quality, naturally grown tomato products and farming knowledge, inspired by Uganda's rich savanna landscape.

Possible domain: savannasweetness.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate and support farmers, gardeners, and consumers with detailed information about tomato varieties, growing tips, and post-harvest handling practices. The website will provide resources such as planting guides, pest control methods, and market advice to help improve tomato yields and quality. It also aims to showcase best practices, success stories, and new innovations in tomato farming.

Scenarios

  • What are the best tomato varieties for growing in Uganda's climate?
  • How can I control pests naturally in my tomato garden?

Color Scheme

  • Tomato Red (#E53935): Used for headings, buttons, and call-to-action elements.
  • Leaf Green (#43A047):Used for backgrounds, borders, and accents.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Used for main headings and titles (adds elegance)
  • Open Sans: Used for body text and paragraphs (clean and readable)
